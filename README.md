# TaxHelp.lk – Professional Tax Consultancy Platform

![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Live-success)
![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/Platform-Tecpins%20Web%20Solutions-blue)

**TaxHelp.lk** is a high-performance, professional tax consultancy website developed for a leading Sri Lankan firm specializing in individual and business taxation. The platform is designed to highlight expert services and streamline lead generation for tax compliance and business registration.

🔗 **Live Website:** [http://taxhelp.lk/](http://taxhelp.lk/)

---

## 🛠 Technology Stack
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap (Responsive UI)
* **Backend:** PHP
* **Database:** MySQL
* **Deployment:** Secure Cloud Hosting, SEO Optimization

---

## ✨ Key Features
* **Responsive Modern UI:** Optimized for a seamless experience across desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
* **Industry-Specific Modules:** Dedicated sections for Tax Consultancy, Business Registration, and Corporate Compliance.
* **Lead Generation:** Integrated contact forms designed to capture client inquiries efficiently.
* **SEO Optimized:** Structured content and meta-tags to ensure high visibility on Google search results in Sri Lanka.
* **Security & Speed:** Fast-loading pages with a secure hosting configuration.
